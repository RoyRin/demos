LLM-powered Arithmetic Compression

Arithmetic coding with LLMs (live demo). With error correction in the logits to account for non-determinism!

Inference Verification (in a TEE!)

"Inference Verification" is the task of verify that tokens came from a model. Check out a live demo for running inference verification in a TEE - to provide hardware-enabled cryptographic guarantees that tokens really did come from a specific model.

Question-Asking Compression

Live demo for "Question-Asking" Compression - a novel technique we invented in our paper which enables the compression of complex text in just 10 bits through the power of question asking! Check out the demo for a live demo of Haiku asking Opus questions.
